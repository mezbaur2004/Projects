The Corsair Katar Pro Wired Mouse is a gaming mouse designed for high-performance gaming. It features a lightweight design for enhanced maneuverability and control during gaming sessions. The mouse is equipped with a high-precision optical sensor with a maximum DPI of 12,400, ensuring accurate tracking and responsiveness. With six programmable buttons, users can customize the mouse to their preferences and assign macros or functions for quick access. The ambidextrous design makes it comfortable for both right-handed and left-handed users, accommodating different grip styles and hand sizes. The Katar Pro also offers customizable RGB lighting, allowing users to personalize the aesthetics of their gaming setup. With onboard profile storage, users can save their customized settings directly on the mouse and easily switch between different computers without the need for additional software installation. The mouse is built to be durable, ensuring long-lasting performance even during intense gaming sessions. The wired connectivity of the Katar Pro provides a stable and lag-free connection, making it ideal for competitive gaming. Overall, the Corsair Katar Pro Wired Mouse offers a lightweight and customizable gaming experience with high-performance features and a durable build quality.
